flipped `assignment_kind` to `variance` because the always-on variance drop zone was a nested

## Critical operational gotchas

| No HMR / no --reload anywhere in the wave1 stack | Edits don't go live until restart | `docker restart accu-mk1-frontend` after FE edits (Vite :3101); `docker restart accu-mk1-backend` after BE edits (:8012); `docker restart integration-service` after IS edits (:8000, bind-mounts the workspace IS worktree → `/app`, no --reload) |
